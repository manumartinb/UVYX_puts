UVXY Options Research

Systematic studies on UVXY put strategies under different futures curve regimes.

This repository contains backtests and diagnostics on long and short UVXY put strategies, with all results adjusted for historical reverse splits. Performance is evaluated using FWD 90% PnL, focusing on near-expiration outcomes.

  • Long Put UVXY — FWD 90% PnL Analysis
  • Short Put UVXY — FWD 90% PnL Analysis
  • Short Put UVXY — Edge Diagnostic & Regime Breakdown

All results are research-oriented and do not account for transaction costs, slippage, margin constraints, or intra-trade drawdowns.
